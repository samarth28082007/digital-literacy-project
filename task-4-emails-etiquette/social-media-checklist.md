## Responsible Social Media Use Checklist (For College Students)

###  Do's
-  Think before posting — ensure content is respectful and appropriate  
-  Maintain privacy — protect personal and sensitive information  
-  Verify information before sharing — avoid spreading misinformation  
-  Communicate respectfully — be polite in comments and discussions  
-  Build a positive online presence — share meaningful and constructive content  

###  Don'ts
-  Do not share confidential or personal details publicly  
-  Do not post offensive, harmful, or inappropriate content  
-  Do not spread rumors or unverified information  
-  Do not engage in cyberbullying or toxic arguments  
-  Do not overuse social media — avoid distraction and addiction
