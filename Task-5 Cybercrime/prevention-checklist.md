## Stay Safe Online-Checklist
# General Safety Tips
 Use strong, unique passwords for each account (mix letters, numbers, symbols)
 Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on email, social media, and banking apps
 Do not click on suspicious links or unknown attachments in emails or messages
 Keep your apps and devices updated to avoid security vulnerabilities
 Avoid using public Wi-Fi for logging into important accounts
 Review and limit app permissions (camera, contacts, location) regularly
 Think before sharing personal information like phone number, address, or college details
 Log out from shared/public computers and devices
 # UPI & Financial Safety
 Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or banking details with anyone
 Do not accept unknown “Collect Requests” or scan QR codes sent by strangers
 Verify payment requests carefully—even if they appear from known contacts
 # Reporting Cybercrime
 Report incidents immediately at National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
 Call Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 for urgent financial fraud assistance
